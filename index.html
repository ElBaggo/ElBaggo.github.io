Services

Loft Ladder Installation

We offer a range of loft ladders for installation. Check the Loft Ladders section to see pictures of each of the ladders as well as their specifications and prices. The prices listed include the cost of fitting the ladder to an existing loft hatch. If you don't already have a loft hatch or have an existing hatch in need of modification, a new hatchway can be installed for an additional fee (Provided it is possible). If you are interested in having a loft-ladder installed, contact us and we will send a joiner to give a free quotation. If you want to go ahead, the joiner can fit the loft ladder there and then. Fitting the ladder usually takes a few hours and you are under no obligation to have the ladder fitted if you are not completely satisfied.

Loft Boarding

If you require some boarding in your loft to facilitate easier and safer use, our usual method is to lay 3' 0" x 2 '0" timber boards over the existing ceiling joists. This raises the floor level above any plumbing and electrics and permits a layer of insulation to be left in place.

Loft Hatch Installation

Should you require a new or enlarged hatchway, we can fit a UPVC (plastic) or timber hatchway made to suit your requirements. The Eco model of loft ladder comes complete with a loft hatch and therefore, having an Eco ladder fitted can work out cheaper than having a new hatchway made. Sometimes it is easier and more cost effective to locate the hatch in another room than it is to modify an existing hatchway. The joiner will discuss your options with you at the quotation.

Velux Window Installation

Velux® roof windows start at ~ £350 for a small Velux window depending on the roof covering and access. As this is a more unique service, please call for further details.
